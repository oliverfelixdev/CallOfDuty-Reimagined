Multiplayer

In Black Ops 6 Multiplayer, players will test their skills across 16 new maps at launch, including 12 core 6v6 maps and 4 Strike maps that can be played 2v2 or 6v6. Additionally, players will see the return of a more traditional Prestige system, bigger and more rewarding than ever. Post-launch, players can look forward to even more exciting maps and groundbreaking experiences dropping into both Multiplayer and Zombies.

Call of Duty: Black Ops6 is the latest installment in the iconic Black Ops series, renowned for its intense, fast-paced action and gripping storyline. This game takes players deeper into covert operations with cutting-edge weaponry, advanced tactical gameplay, and a narrative filled with twists and turns. The multiplayer mode offers a variety of new maps, modes, and customization options, keeping the competitive spirit alive. Whether you're battling through the single-player campaign or diving into the expansive multiplayer arenas, Black Ops 6 promises an immersive experience that stays true to the legacy of the franchise while pushing the boundaries of what fans can expect.
